# Symbolic Notes And Lyric Fragments (2019-2025)

## Overview

A collection of lyric fragments, symbolic imagery, emotional motifs, and recurring thematic structures written prior to and alongside the development of the Reality Drift framework.

These writings are not part of the formal framework itself, but preserve recurring symbolic and emotional patterns that later appeared conceptually in Reality Drift, Cognitive Drift, and Drifted Identity.

---
