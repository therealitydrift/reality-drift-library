# Symbolic Notes And Lyric Fragments (2019–2024)

A collection of lyric fragments, symbolic imagery, emotional motifs, and recurring thematic structures written prior to and alongside the development of the Reality Drift framework.

This folder preserves a symbolic and emotional continuity layer rather than formal theoretical writing.

The material captures recurring themes involving:

- drift
- distance
- overstimulation
- modern numbness
- fragmented identity
- longing for grounded life
- nostalgia
- becoming
- disconnection
- meaning loss
- modern alienation

These writings are not intended as canonical framework documents. Instead, they preserve recurring symbolic patterns and emotional intuitions that later appeared conceptually in:

- Reality Drift
- Cognitive Drift
- Drifted Identity
- Filter Fatigue
- Synthetic Meaning
