# AI Governance

A collection of governance frameworks, readiness assessments, operational checklists, and policy-oriented materials related to AI systems.

These documents focus on the practical challenge of maintaining alignment after deployment. They examine how organizations monitor, evaluate, constrain, and correct AI systems as they become embedded within real workflows and decision environments.

Topics include AI governance readiness, drift detection, semantic fidelity, observability, post-deployment oversight, vendor evaluation, workflow integration, and organizational correction mechanisms.

The materials in this section are intended for leaders, governance teams, risk managers, policy practitioners, and organizations seeking practical approaches to managing AI systems over time.

Contents may include:

- AI Governance Readiness Checklists

- Drift Detection Frameworks

- Semantic Fidelity Audits

- AI Observability Guides

- Vendor Evaluation Frameworks

- Post-Deployment Governance Models

- Operational Governance Templates
