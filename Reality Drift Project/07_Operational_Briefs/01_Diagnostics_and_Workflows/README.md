# Diagnostics and Workflows

Part of the Reality Drift framework (2023–2026) by A. Jacobs

This section contains practical tools for identifying and reducing misalignment in decision systems.

These documents operate at the applied layer of the Reality Drift framework. They are designed to move from recognizing drift to structuring how decisions are made under conditions of incomplete or indirect information.

---

## Included

- **Are You Optimizing the Proxy?**  
  A diagnostic for identifying when systems optimize what is being measured instead of what is actually happening.

- **Cognitive Workflows: Avoiding Proxy Optimization**  
  A structured reasoning process that reduces the likelihood of selecting answers that are coherent but misaligned with reality.

- **Reality-Constrained Systems**  
  A framework for designing systems that maintain alignment under optimization pressure through anchors, constraints, and diagnostics.

---

## How These Fit Together

These documents form a progression:

1. **Diagnostic**  
   Identify when proxy optimization is occurring.

2. **Workflow**  
   Structure reasoning to reduce misalignment.

3. **System Design**  
   Embed constraints so alignment is maintained consistently.

Taken together, they move from detection to correction to prevention.

---

## When to Use

These tools are most useful when:

- decisions rely on metrics, proxies, or indirect signals  
- outputs appear correct but feel slightly off  
- systems are being optimized, scaled, or automated  
- performance improves without clear real-world improvement  

In these conditions, it becomes easy to optimize representations rather than reality.

---

## Relation to the Framework

This section applies core concepts from the Reality Drift framework:

- **Reality Drift** — loss of alignment between representation and reality  
- **Proxy Optimization** — optimizing what is measured instead of what matters  
- **Semantic Fidelity** — preservation of meaning across transformation  

These documents translate those concepts into practical methods for reasoning and system design.
