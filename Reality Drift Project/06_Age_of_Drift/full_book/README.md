## Overview

This directory contains the complete book as a single PDF.

*The Age of Drift* examines a structural pattern emerging across modern systems:

> systems can continue functioning while gradually losing alignment with reality.

This pattern is described as **Reality Drift**.

Across media, institutions, and AI systems, outputs become optimized, coherent, and persuasive — while drifting further from the conditions they are meant to represent.

## What This Book Covers

- Why modern life increasingly feels artificial or unreal  
- How optimization and proxy metrics distort systems  
- The collapse of attention and meaning under information overload  
- How human cognition adapts under algorithmic pressure  
- Why AI systems can appear correct while lacking grounding  
- The deeper structure behind these patterns
