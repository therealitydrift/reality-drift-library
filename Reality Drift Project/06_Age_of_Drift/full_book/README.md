## Archive Note

This directory contains the complete original manuscript of *The Age of Drift*. The book was written between 2023 and 2024 and preserves an early stage in the development of the Reality Drift framework.

It contains formative essays, conceptual experiments, and early structural observations that later evolved into the canonical framework.

While many of the core ideas remain active, several definitions, boundaries, and relationships have since been revised, clarified, or formalized elsewhere.

This file is maintained as a historical artifact of the framework’s emergence.

---

## Overview

*The Age of Drift* explores an increasingly visible structural pattern:

> systems can continue functioning while gradually losing alignment with reality.

This observation became the foundation for what would later be formalized as Reality Drift.

Across media, institutions, cognition, and AI systems, the manuscript traces how outputs can become optimized, coherent, and persuasive while gradually drifting away from the conditions they are meant to represent.
