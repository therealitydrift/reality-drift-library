# Texture of Modern Life

**README Version: v1**

This folder contains visual studies of how Reality Drift appears in ordinary daily life. These artifacts focus on lived encounters. Small routines, interfaces, systems, and behaviors that increasingly shape modern experience.

These materials document how drift becomes experiential. They are designed to make structural concepts recognizable through familiar situations.

---

## Folder Index

- [Filter-Fatigue](./Filter-Fatigue/)  
  Visual examples of cognitive overload, endless decision layers, and attention fragmentation in everyday systems.

- [Reality-Drift](./Reality-Drift/)  
  Ordinary procedural experiences where systems remain functional while becoming increasingly complex, abstracted, or detached from lived reality.

- [Synthetic-Realness](./Synthetic-Realness/)  
  Everyday scenes where authenticity is simulated, performed, or mediated through representational layers.

---

## Function

These visuals serve as:

- Recognition tools for lived drift  
- Everyday examples of larger framework mechanics  
- Entry points for non-technical audiences  
- Applied illustrations of abstraction in ordinary life  

They focus on texture rather than theory.

The goal is not explanation first, but recognition.

---

## Structural Role

This folder acts as the experiential layer of the Reality Drift archive.

Where canonical papers define drift structurally, these visuals show what drift feels like from inside the system.
