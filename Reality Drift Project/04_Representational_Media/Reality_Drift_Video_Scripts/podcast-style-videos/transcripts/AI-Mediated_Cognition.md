# AI-Mediated Cognition

## Overview

AI is changing more than how we access information. It is changing how thought itself is formed.

This explainer introduces AI-Mediated Cognition, Co-Cognition, Cognitive Drift, Cognitive Debt, and the emerging role of AI as an exocortex. It examines how human cognition increasingly operates through recursive interaction with external intelligence systems.

## Main Takeaways

### 1. Thought Is Becoming Distributed

Human cognition increasingly emerges through interaction with external systems rather than purely internal reasoning.

### 2. AI Functions As Cognitive Infrastructure

AI is becoming part of the environment within which thinking occurs.

### 3. Memory Is Shifting From Retention To Retrieval

The challenge is increasingly access, reconstruction, and synthesis rather than storage.

### 4. Cognitive Drift Is A Structural Risk

Fast recursive feedback can weaken grounding and distort internal models.

### 5. Adaptation Matters More Than Avoidance

The question is not whether AI will participate in cognition. The question is how that participation remains aligned with reality.
