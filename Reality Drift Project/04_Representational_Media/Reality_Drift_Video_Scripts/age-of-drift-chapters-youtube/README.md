# Age of Drift Chapters (YouTube)

**README Version: v1**

This folder contains video adaptations of selected chapters from *The Age of Drift*. These materials translate early book chapters into spoken-form scripts and visual video assets for YouTube distribution. They preserve the original chapter logic while extending the material into an audio-visual format.

---

## Chapter Index

- Chapter 1 — Everything Feels Fake Now 
  A foundational introduction to the broader drift condition and why modern life increasingly feels unreal.

- Chapter 5 — Filter Fatigue and the Collapse of Attention  
  An early exploration of cognitive overload and endless filtration.

- Chapter 10 — The Drift Principle  
  A structural model describing how systems gradually lose contact with reality without visible failure.

- Chapter 14 — The Drifted Self  
  A reflection on identity formation under recursive symbolic environments.

- Chapter 15 — Institutional Drift  
  How organizations preserve form while losing meaning.

- Chapter 16 — The Fidelity Crisis in AI Alignment  
  Early thinking on semantic fidelity, alignment failure, and representational degradation in language systems.

---

## Function

These materials serve as:

- Long-form narrative video adaptations  
- Public-facing chapter translations  
- Historical bridge material between book and framework  
- Early archive artifacts documenting conceptual development  

---

## Archive Note

These chapter scripts reflect early framework development. While many core ideas remain active, later canonical papers refine, narrow, and formalize several of the concepts introduced here. They should be understood as developmental source material.
