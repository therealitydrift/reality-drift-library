## Podcast Style Videos

This folder contains longform discussion and podcast-style videos exploring concepts from the Reality Drift framework, cognitive drift research, semantic fidelity, institutional drift, AI-mediated cognition, optimization systems, and modern informational environments.

Unlike shorter explainer clips, these videos are designed as extended conceptual discussions that move through interconnected ideas, cultural patterns, structural mechanisms, and cognitive dynamics over time.

Topics include:

- Reality Drift  
- Synthetic Realness  
- Optimization Trap  
- Cognitive Drift  
- AI-Mediated Cognition  
- Institutional Drift  
- Semantic Fidelity  
- Filter Fatigue  
- Constraint Collapse  
- The Age of Drift  

These videos function as conversational explorations rather than highly compressed educational summaries, allowing larger ontological and cultural relationships to emerge across the discussion itself.
