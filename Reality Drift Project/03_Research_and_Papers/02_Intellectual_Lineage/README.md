# Intellectual Lineage — Reality Drift Framework

This section maps the conceptual lineage of the Reality Drift framework to prior thinkers whose work explored related patterns in systems, language, and cognition.

These documents are not summaries. They are structural comparisons that show how earlier ideas connect to the mechanisms described in Reality Drift.

## Included Papers

- **Reality Drift and Gregory Bateson**  
  Explores feedback loops, double binds, and how systems lose the ability to self-correct.

- **Reality Drift and Marshall McLuhan**  
  Connects media environments to representational distortion and the shaping of perception.

- **Reality Drift and Terrence Deacon**  
  Examines emergence, constraints, and the relationship between symbols and physical reality.
