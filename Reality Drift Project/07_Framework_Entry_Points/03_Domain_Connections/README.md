## Cross-Domain Applications of the Reality Drift Framework

### Overview

This directory contains short papers applying the Reality Drift framework across different institutional, technological, and social domains. These materials examine how similar structural patterns emerge across environments where optimization pressures, mediation layers, symbolic abstraction, and feedback distortion begin to replace direct contact with underlying reality.

Each paper focuses on a specific domain while connecting it to broader systemic dynamics described throughout the Reality Drift framework.

## Role Within Reality Drift Explained

Cross-domain pattern papers demonstrate how similar structural dynamics can emerge across very different systems and environments. They are intended to help readers recognize recurring mechanisms of drift, optimization, and representational instability operating beneath modern institutional and technological systems.
